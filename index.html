Locomotive's Suggested Travel Destinations

The best travel destinations designed for you and your loved ones!

Munnar

Munnar, Kerala, is a paradise of lush green tea plantations, misty waterfalls, and serene lakes, offering breathtaking views of the Western Ghats. Its pristine valleys and exotic flora and fauna make it a must-visit destination for nature.

Esimated Travel cost : ₹7,000-₹12,000 Must Visit
Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi, is a city of spiritual and cultural significance. With its sacred Ganges River and ancient temples, it’s a place where history and spirituality blend beautifully.

Estimated Travel cost : ₹6,000-₹9,000SpiritualEconomical
Mumbai

Mumbai, the city that never sleeps, is a vibrant blend of cultures and lifestyles. From the iconic Gateway of India to the bustling streets of Colaba, it offers a unique mix of history, modernity, and endless energy.

Estimated Travel cost : ₹10,000-₹15,000Popular
Jaipur

Jaipur, the Pink City, enchants with its regal palaces and vibrant architecture, where every street is bathed in hues of history and culture. From the majestic Amer Fort to the bustling bazaars, the city's timeless charm captures the heart of all who visit.

Estimated Travel cost : ₹6,000-₹10,000Economical
Goa

Goa, with its sun-kissed beaches and lively nightlife, is a tropical paradise where every sunset feels magical. Its blend of Portuguese heritage and vibrant culture makes it a haven for both relaxation and adventure.

Estimated Travel cost : ₹8,000-₹12,000Popular
Rishikesh

Rishikesh, nestled along the banks of the sacred Ganges, is a serene haven for yoga, meditation, and spiritual seekers. Surrounded by the majestic Himalayas, it's also an adventure hub, offering thrilling experiences like river rafting and trekking.

Estimated Travel cost : ₹6,000-₹10,000Spiritual
